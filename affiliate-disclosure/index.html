Affiliate Disclosure

Last Updated: January 2025

Transparency is a core value at EmergencyKitGuide. This page explains how affiliate relationships work on our site, how they support our mission, and our commitment to honest, unbiased recommendations.

What Are Affiliate Links

Affiliate links are special URLs that contain a unique tracking code assigned to us by a retailer or partner. When you click on an affiliate link on our site and make a purchase, the retailer can identify that the referral came from EmergencyKitGuide.

Affiliate links look like regular links and behave the same way from your perspective. The only difference is that they contain a tracking parameter that allows the retailer to attribute the sale to our referral.

How Affiliate Links Work

Here is the step-by-step process:

  1. You read one of our guides, reviews, or articles that includes a link to a product or service.
  2. When you click that link, a cookie is placed on your browser by the retailer (not by us) to track the referral.
  3. If you make a purchase from the retailer within a specified time window (typically 24 hours to 30 days, depending on the retailer), the sale is attributed to our referral.
  4. The retailer pays us a small commission as a referral fee.

The price you pay is exactly the same whether you use our affiliate link or go directly to the retailer. The commission comes from the retailer's marketing budget, not from your purchase price. In some cases, you may even receive a discount by using our links.

Our Affiliate Partners

We participate in affiliate programs offered by retailers and platforms where we purchase and evaluate emergency preparedness products. These may include, but are not limited to:

  • Major online retailers (such as Amazon Associates)
  • Specialty outdoor and survival gear stores
  • Safety equipment manufacturers and distributors
  • First aid and medical supply retailers

Our participation in these programs does not influence which products we choose to review or recommend. We select products based on their quality, value, and relevance to our readers.

Product Recommendations

Every product recommendation on EmergencyKitGuide goes through a thorough evaluation process:

  • Research: We identify leading products in each category based on industry standards, expert opinions, and consumer feedback.
  • Testing: Our team evaluates products for quality, durability, functionality, and value for money.
  • Review: We compare products against alternatives and assess how well they meet the needs of people preparing for emergencies.
  • Recommendation: Only products that meet our standards are recommended to our readers.

The presence or absence of an affiliate relationship with a product's retailer has no bearing on whether we include it in our recommendations. We have recommended and will continue to recommend products from retailers with whom we have no affiliate relationship.

Our Promise

We make the following commitments to our readers:

  • Honest Reviews: Our reviews, ratings, and recommendations are always based on our genuine assessment of each product's quality and value, regardless of any affiliate relationship.
  • No Pay-for-Play: We do not accept payment in exchange for positive reviews, rankings, or placement on our site. Sponsored content, if any, will be clearly labeled.
  • Editorial Independence: Affiliate partners have no influence over our editorial content, product rankings, or the advice we provide.
  • Reader-First Approach: We always prioritize the safety and well-being of our readers over potential affiliate revenue.

Your trust is the foundation of our site. We would rather lose a commission than recommend a product we do not believe in.

FTC Compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's (FTC) guidelines, we disclose our affiliate relationships to ensure full transparency with our readers. The FTC requires that material connections between endorsers and advertisers be clearly disclosed.

We comply with these requirements by:

  • Disclosing our affiliate relationships on this page and within individual articles where affiliate links appear
  • Using clear language such as "As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases" or similar disclosures near affiliate links
  • Providing this comprehensive Affiliate Disclosure page accessible from every page of our site
  • Reviewing our disclosures regularly to ensure compliance with the latest FTC guidance

We believe in full transparency and are committed to going beyond the minimum legal requirements to ensure our readers understand how our site is funded.
